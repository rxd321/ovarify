Features used by Machine Learning Model (Random Forest)

CEA The CEA test, also known as the Carcinoembryonic Antigen test, is used to measure the amount of CEA protein in the bloodstream (measured in ng/ml)
IBIL A IBIL test determines the total amount of bilirubin in the body. Bilirubin produced by the breakdown of red blood cells is known as indirect bilirubin (measure in mg/dl)
NEU Neutrophils are the most common white blood cells, accounting for 50-70% of all white blood cells. The relative neutrophil count, which appears on your CBC, is this percentage
Menopause Menopause describes the period in which a woman’s ovaries stop the production of hormones, therefore also terminating menstruation. This condition is diagnosed after an individual has gone 12 months without menstruation
CA125 CA-125 is a protein that is produced on the surface of cells and released into the bloodstream. More than 80% of women with advanced ovarian cancer and 50% of those with early-stage tumors have high levels of this protein. A high level of CA125 in the bloodstream can be indicative of Ovarian Cancer (measured in units/ml)
ALB Albumin is a protein present in the bloodstream. An albumin blood test is a type of liver function test - a blood test that measures various enzymes and proteins in the liver (measured in g/l)
GLO Globulins are a type of protein found in the bloodstream that are produced by your immune system in your liver. Globulins are involved in liver function, blood coagulation, and defense against infection (measured in g/l)
LYM% Lymphocytes constitute about 20 to 40 percent of the total number of white blood cells. This percentage is what you read in your CBC as the Relative Lymphocytes Count
HE4 The HE4 test determines how much human epididymis protein 4 is present in the blood. Significantly higher levels of HE4 are present in the bloodstream of a woman with Ovarian Cancer (measured in pmol/l)
